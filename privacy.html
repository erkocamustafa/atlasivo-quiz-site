Privacy Policy

  • Last updated: 29 September 2025

    • We keep our analytics simple. We only measure quiz completions to understand which quizzes are popular and how results are distributed. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers.

    What we collect

    • Quiz completion events (anonymized): quiz_id, result_id, and a timestamp. For some quizzes we also record a non-identifying completion duration.
    • No IP addresses are stored in our analytics tables. No precise location, device identifiers, or fingerprinting are used.

    How we use this data

    We aggregate counts to show “Most Solved” quizzes and to improve content (e.g., result distribution and overall interest). Data is used only for functionality and quality metrics, not for advertising or profiling.

    Cookies & third parties

    • No advertising cookies and no cross-site tracking.
    • Our basic stats run on our own server-side counter (via Cloudflare Workers + D1). If we ever add optional third-party analytics, we will update this page first.

    Data retention

    Event records are kept in aggregate form for trend analysis. We may periodically prune raw events while keeping aggregated counts.

    Sharing

    We do not sell or share analytics data with third parties for marketing purposes.
